Hi, I'm lee, a student at Eastern Washington University. I grew up in Spokane WA, where I attended District 81 for my education until I graduated from Lewis & Clark High School in 2024. I'm majoring in visual communication design so I can further my skills in design and visual development. I'm proficient in using Adobe creative programs such as Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, and other creative programs such as Figma and Github. I also have experience with physical media such as poster making, risograph printing, and paper modeling. I enjoy reading, listening to music, walking, playing Dungeon and Dragons (or watching other people play it), and hanging out with my friends.
