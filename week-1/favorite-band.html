The Crane Wives

The crane wives are a 4 member indie folk band that started in the early 2010s. Their songs showcase both musical talent and an exploration of the uglier and vulnerable parts of humanity. They've grown a significant following, having 1.5 million streamers on spotify and many accolades from fans and critics alike.
