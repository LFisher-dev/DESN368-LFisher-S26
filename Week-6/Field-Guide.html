General Information

Overwatch is a First Person Shooter (FPS) co-op game first launched in 2016. It later relaunched as Overwatch 2 in 2022, however most of the core mechanics are the same (with slight alterations). For example, both OW1 & 2 have you engaging in player vs player combat on a team; however, OW2 has decreased the team count from 6 players to 5 per team.

Heroes

The game's most popular feature is their roster of playable characters in the game also known as their Heroes. As of 2026, there are currently 51 heroes total; 32 from Overwatch 1 and 19 additional heroes since Overwatch 2 launched.

There are 3 primary classes of heroes, and each class has 3-4 subclasses, each giving their team a bonus passive.

  • Tanks: Your main damage takers; they should be leading the charge for the team.
  • DPS or Damage: Your main damage dealer; they seek out, engage, and eliminate the enemy.
  • Support: Your main healers, shield-providers, and damage boosters; if your support survives, your team does too.
