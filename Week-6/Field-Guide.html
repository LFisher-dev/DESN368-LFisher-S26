General Information

Overwatch (often abbreviated to OW) is a First Person Shooter (FPS) co-op game first launched in 2016. It later relaunched as Overwatch 2 in 2022, however most of the core mechanics are the same (with slight alterations). For example, both OW1 & 2 have you engaging in player vs player combat on a team; however, OW2 has decreased the team count from 6 players to 5 per team.

Heroes

The game's most popular feature is their roster of playable characters in the game also known as their Heroes. As of 2026, there are currently 51 heroes total; 32 from Overwatch 1 and 19 additional heroes since Overwatch 2 launched.

There are 3 primary classes of heroes, and each class has 3-4 subclasses, each giving the player a bonus passive.

  • Tanks: Your main damage takers; they should be leading the charge for the team.
    • Buisers: Heroes like , Roadhog, and Zarya gain resistance to critical hits and move faster while below half health.
    • Initiators: Heroes like D.VA, Doomfist, and Winston heal overtime while staying airborne.
    • Stalwarts: Heroes like Junker Queen, Rammatra, and Reinhardt gain resistance to knockback and being slowed.
  • DPS or Damage: Your main damage dealer; they seek out, engage, and eliminate the enemy.
    • Flankers: Heroes like Genji, Reaper, and Tracer allow health packs on you find on the map restore more health
    • Recons: Heroes like Echo, Sombra, and Pharah reveal enemies below half health
    • Sharpshooters: Heroes like Ashe, Hanzo, and Widowmaker cause critical hits to reduce your movement ability cooldown.
    • Specialists: Heroes like Bastion, Mei, and Soldier: 76 increase your reload speed upon eliminating an enemy.
  • Support: Your main healers, shield-providers, and damage boosters; if your support survives, your team does too.
    • Medics: Heroes like Mercy, Moira, and Kiriko allow healing with your weapon to heal yourself
    • Survivors: Heroes like Brigitte, Illari, and Juno cause your movement abilities to start passive health regeneration.
    • Tactitions: Heroes like Ana, Baptiste, and Zenyatta store excess ultimate charge.
