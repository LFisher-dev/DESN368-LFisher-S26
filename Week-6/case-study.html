Overwatch Field Guide

Design Choices

What design choices did you make & why?

One of the things I really appreciate about Overwatch from a design perspective is how it has a simple yet iconic color palette. You have your cool neutrals while keeping the orange has a striking accent colors. Additionally, since my field guide was inspired by the UIX design of the settings, it also included a striking teal for interactive items (ie navigation). The one thing I did struggle with (slightly) was the choice of typography as I couldn't find the font Overwatch uses (Kenyan Coffee according to online forums) as a google font. Luckily, I was able to work around this; understanding a recurring motif in Overwatch's overall design are sci-fi/technology aesthetics, I was able to find Science Gothic for my section headers and Mozilla Text for the navigation text.

One thing I truly wanted to emulate was their navigation system, very spaced out wherein it would highlight teal when interacting with it. The navigation system and the gradient background for the page overall are what I took directly from the game design wise. As for the sections, I wanted to keep it very simple with just a blue-grey margin strip at the top for an accent color. In the end, I didn't quite know where to place the orange, so I decidd to use it for the header at the top of the page.
